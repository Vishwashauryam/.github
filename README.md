## About Us
The Vishwashauryam Defense Club at Vishwakarma Institute of Technology, Pune, is a student-driven initiative dedicated to guiding and preparing individuals aspiring for defense-oriented careers. While the club focuses on personality development, discipline, and fostering leadership qualities, the Technical Wing extends these values into the field of technology and innovation.

## Our Mission
We aim to bridge defense knowledge with modern technology by building applications, tools, and systems that reflect real-world defense challenges. Our goal is to create an environment where students can learn, collaborate, and contribute to projects that enhance problem-solving, strategic thinking, and technical capability.

## What We Do
## Technical Wing Responsibilities
- Develop engineering and technology-based projects aligned with defense principles.
- Provide technical support for various events and activities of the club.
- Build systems that promote awareness, preparedness, and structured training for aspiring defense candidates.
- Encourage students to work on long-term, impactful projects under guided mentorship.

## Focus Areas
- Software and hardware development  
- Research-driven defense technologies  

## Who Can Join
Students who are passionate about defense, technology, or both. Whether your interest lies in leadership, innovation, or contributing to national security through engineering, the club offers a structured platform to learn and grow.

## Vision
To establish a technologically strong and strategically disciplined student community that contributes meaningfully to defense-oriented learning and innovation.

## Connect With Us
To collaborate, contribute to projects, or join the Technical Wing, reach out to the core committee or submit a request through our official club channels.
